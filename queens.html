Friends of Queen's Wood

Design, Development, Mobile

'Friends of Queen's Woods' is a group dedicated looking after the woods and its wildlife. Their current website, originally created in 2007(!), is in dire need of re-designing.

The purpose of the website is to inform vistors on facilities, events and wildlife in the woods. This requires several pages and as a result, the navigation on the current website is cluttered. I combined multiply, related links into a drop down menu under one button on the navigation.

For the history page I wanted each year to have some significance so i used full window images.

Working pages: Home, History and Gallery.
